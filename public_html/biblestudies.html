Revelation: Sept 2015 - June 2016

As we wrapped up our last study for Revelations, Clay reflected on the bright morning star in Revelations 22. “I, Jesus, have sent my angel to testify to you about these things for the churches. I am the root and the descendant of David, the bright morning star” reminding us once again that things are not as they seem. Just as a small star is enough to pull in the morning, Jesus is able to pull the kingdom into this dark and broken world. In similar ways, we too can “enter the darkness of night with the light of the day”. Though we live in a world that is full of sin, the light of Christ shines through each of us and thus we can have significant impact in reaching those who are hurting and everyone who needs Jesus. Thank you for joining our 2015-2016 study, exploring what it means to do “discipleship on the edge”.
